Pudge

 
        Each carefully aimed throw of Pudge's signature Meat Hook strikes fear
        into his foes. Disoriented, his foe stumbles, gagging on a sudden
        stench of Rot. As it looks up at the fleshy hulk, hoping for an answer,
        only to watch as the Butcher Dismembers and carves them with his
        cleavers, adding a layer to his compost Heap. With every kill he gains
        additional health and damage, and soon becomes an unstoppable
        behemoth of rotting flesh.
